1. Prepare at least 3 sets of questions,
   for example: animals, math, food

2. In each set at least 3 questions

(the sets and the questions could be 
very simple)

3. The player will choose a set of the questions

4. When he gets question he can answer
or skip or stop the game

5. He should get some score for the right answer,
and loose some score for the wrong answer
